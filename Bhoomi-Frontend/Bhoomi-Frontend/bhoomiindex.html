Why Bhoomi?

There are many opportunities for urban women to earn and to keep upadted about the latest news but rural women often left behind due to lack of proper services and guidance. This website helps women to stay updated about latest news and services. Not only earning money but this platform also helps women to raise complaint against any kind of discomfort or misbehave.

OUR MOTTO

Our main aim is to fill the gap between rural and urban societies and to expand rural heritage both locally and among urban too. It also keeps in mind to utilise the skills of rural women and help them earn money and also to stay updated about latest news and to protect them from any kind of mishappening and misbehave. This website enables women to upload specific handmade products like pickles, snacks, knitted clothing, paintings,etc and also services that they can provide.

How We Came Up With This Idea?

Bhoomi is about the development of rural women and helping them to earn on their own by selling their hand-made products. In many circumstances women are often not allowed to earn on their own and make living even if she wishes to. Most common reason being no support or 'permission' granted by family. Considering these issues we came up with the solution of giving them this opportunity to use their skills to make a living and to stand on their own feet. It is accessible on mobile phones also. This also has a feature which ensure women to file complaint against any kind of mishappening or misbehave.
